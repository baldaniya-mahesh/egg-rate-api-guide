# India Egg Rate Data Guide

This repository contains a simple technical guide for understanding India egg rate data, NECC egg rate references, city-wise egg prices, tray rate, peti rate, and poultry market price updates.

Egg price data is useful for poultry farmers, egg wholesalers, food businesses, restaurants, hotels, bakeries, and market researchers who want to understand daily egg price movement in India.

## What Is Egg Rate Data?

Egg rate data usually includes:

- 1 egg price
- Egg tray rate
- Egg peti rate
- City-wise egg price
- State-wise egg price
- NECC egg rate reference
- Daily market movement
- Previous day price comparison

Egg prices can change due to demand, supply, poultry feed cost, transport cost, weather conditions, and local wholesale market activity.

## Useful Egg Rate Resource

For updated city-wise egg prices in India, you can refer to:

[Egg Rate Today](https://egg-rate.in/)

This resource provides egg price updates for different Indian cities and helps users check daily egg market trends.
